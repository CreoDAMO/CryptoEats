Independent Contractor Agreement

CryptoEats Delivery Partner Agreement

This Independent Contractor Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between CryptoEats LLC ("Company"), a Florida limited liability company, and the individual accepting this Agreement through the CryptoEats Driver App ("Contractor").

1. Independent Contractor Relationship

Contractor is engaged as an independent contractor, not an employee of Company. Nothing in this Agreement creates an employer-employee, partnership, joint venture, or agency relationship. Contractor acknowledges that:

  • Contractor controls their own schedule and is free to accept or decline any delivery request
  • Contractor may perform services for other companies simultaneously, including competitors
  • Contractor provides their own vehicle, smartphone, equipment, and supplies at their own expense
  • Contractor is responsible for their own taxes, insurance, vehicle maintenance, fuel, and business expenses
  • Company does not provide benefits, workers' compensation, or unemployment insurance

2. Services

Contractor agrees to:

  • Deliver food and beverage orders from restaurant partners to customers using the CryptoEats Driver App
  • Follow all applicable local, state, and federal laws during deliveries
  • Maintain food safety standards during transport (temperature, handling, sealed containers)
  • Complete age verification procedures for alcohol deliveries as required by Florida law
  • Communicate professionally with customers and restaurant staff

3. Alcohol Delivery Responsibilities

Florida Law Compliance (FS 561.57, FS 565.045, SB 676):

  • Verify customer age (21+) by checking government-issued photo ID at every alcohol delivery
  • Record ID verification in the Driver App before completing delivery
  • Refuse delivery if customer cannot produce valid ID or appears intoxicated
  • Deliver only during permitted hours: 8:00 AM to 10:00 PM Eastern Time
  • Ensure all alcohol containers remain sealed throughout delivery
  • Never deliver alcohol to a minor under any circumstances

Failure to comply with alcohol delivery laws may result in criminal liability for the Contractor, fines from the Florida Division of Alcoholic Beverages and Tobacco, and termination of this Agreement.

4. Compensation

Contractor will receive compensation for completed deliveries as follows:

  • Base Pay: Per-delivery fee as displayed in the Driver App at the time of acceptance
  • Mileage: Distance-based compensation calculated by the platform
  • Tips: 100% of customer tips are passed through to the Contractor
  • Crypto Payments: Contractor may opt to receive earnings in USDC on the Base blockchain
  • NFT Rewards: Eligible for milestone-based NFT rewards through the platform

Company will issue IRS Form 1099-NEC for earnings exceeding $600 in a calendar year. Contractor is solely responsible for reporting and paying all applicable taxes.

5. Requirements

5.1 Eligibility

  • Must be at least 18 years old (21+ for alcohol deliveries)
  • Valid driver's license issued in the United States
  • Valid auto insurance meeting Florida minimum requirements
  • Reliable vehicle in good working condition
  • Smartphone capable of running the CryptoEats Driver App

5.2 Background Check

Contractor consents to a background check conducted through Checkr (our authorized provider). This includes criminal history, motor vehicle records, and identity verification. Contractor must maintain eligibility throughout the term of this Agreement.

5.3 Insurance

Contractor must maintain, at minimum:

  • Personal auto liability insurance meeting Florida's minimum requirements ($10,000 PIP, $10,000 PDL)
  • Proof of insurance must be uploaded to the Driver App and kept current
  • Company does not provide commercial auto insurance for Contractor's vehicle

6. Human-First Policy

CryptoEats is committed to a Human-First approach to its contractor relationships:

  • No Punitive Deactivation: Contractors will not be deactivated for declining deliveries, low acceptance rates, or periods of inactivity
  • Transparent Communication: All platform changes affecting compensation or operations will be communicated in advance
  • Support Hub: Access to dedicated support, wellness resources, appeals process, and educational materials
  • Fair Dispute Resolution: Contractor concerns are addressed through a formal appeals process before any adverse action
  • Earnings Transparency: Full breakdown of all earnings, fees, and deductions available in real-time

7. Conduct and Safety

  • Obey all traffic laws and operate vehicles safely
  • Do not operate a vehicle while under the influence of drugs or alcohol
  • Do not use the CryptoEats App while driving (use voice navigation or pull over)
  • Report any accidents or safety incidents immediately through the app
  • Maintain a professional and respectful demeanor

8. Confidentiality

Contractor agrees to keep confidential all customer information, delivery addresses, order details, and proprietary platform information encountered during deliveries. Customer data may not be used for any purpose outside of completing deliveries through the CryptoEats platform.

9. Termination

Either party may terminate this Agreement at any time, for any reason, with or without notice. Grounds for immediate termination by Company include:

  • Criminal activity or arrest during a delivery
  • Delivery of alcohol to a minor
  • Driving under the influence during a delivery
  • Theft or tampering with orders
  • Harassment of customers, restaurant staff, or other contractors
  • Failure to maintain valid license or insurance
  • Failed background check re-evaluation

Upon termination, Contractor will receive payment for all completed deliveries. Outstanding crypto earnings will be transferred to the Contractor's registered wallet.

10. Dispute Resolution

Disputes arising under this Agreement shall be resolved through binding arbitration administered by the American Arbitration Association (AAA) in Miami-Dade County, Florida. Contractor retains the right to pursue claims in small claims court (under $5,000).

11. Governing Law

This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of Florida. Venue for any legal proceedings shall be Miami-Dade County, Florida.

12. Entire Agreement

This Agreement constitutes the entire understanding between the parties and supersedes all prior agreements. Modifications require written consent of both parties. Digital acceptance through the CryptoEats Driver App constitutes a valid signature.

13. Acknowledgment

By accepting this Agreement through the CryptoEats Driver App during onboarding, Contractor acknowledges that they have read, understood, and agree to all terms herein. Contractor confirms their independent contractor status and their responsibility for compliance with all applicable laws.
