CryptoEats LLC ("we," "us," or "our") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your information when you use the CryptoEats mobile application, website, and API (collectively, the "Service").

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
  • Location Data: GPS coordinates (with your consent) for delivery tracking and driver routing
  • Usage Data: App interactions, page views, feature usage, session duration
  • Log Data: IP address, browser type, access times, error reports

1.3 Information from Third Parties

  • Persona: Identity verification results for age confirmation
  • Checkr: Background check results for drivers
  • Stripe: Payment confirmation and fraud detection signals
  • Base Blockchain: Public transaction records for crypto payments

Payment Card Data: We never store, process, or have access to your full credit/debit card numbers. All card data is transmitted directly to Stripe, our PCI DSS Level 1 compliant payment processor. We only receive a tokenized reference and the last four digits for your records.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Service Delivery: Process orders, coordinate deliveries, facilitate payments
  • Communication: Order updates (email, SMS, push notifications), customer support
  • Legal Compliance: Age verification for alcohol delivery, tax reporting, regulatory requirements
  • Safety and Security: Fraud prevention, identity verification, background checks
  • Improvement: Analytics, performance monitoring (via Sentry), service optimization
  • Blockchain Operations: Process USDC escrow transactions, mint NFT rewards

3.2 Delivery Partners

We share your delivery address and order details with independent delivery contractors to fulfill your orders. We share your name and contact information with restaurant partners as required to prepare your order.

3.3 Legal Obligations

We may disclose information when required by law, court order, or government request, or to protect rights, property, or safety.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. Per SB 676, restaurant partners listed on our platform may contact you regarding your order through our platform.

4. Data Security

  • Encryption in transit (TLS/HTTPS) and at rest
  • PCI DSS compliance via Stripe for all payment data
  • Helmet security headers, XSS/SQL injection protection
  • Adaptive rate limiting and request fingerprinting
  • JWT-based authentication with bcrypt password hashing
  • Access controls limiting data visibility by role
  • Regular security monitoring via Sentry

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data (subject to retention requirements)
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications at any time
  • Data Portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@cryptoeats.net. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Cookies and Tracking

Our web platform uses cookies for functionality (session management, preferences). We use analytics to understand usage patterns. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

8. Children's Privacy

CryptoEats is not directed to children under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If we learn that we have collected information from a child under 18, we will delete that information promptly.

9. Blockchain Transparency

Cryptocurrency transactions on the Base blockchain are public by nature. While we do not publicly link wallet addresses to your identity, anyone can view transaction records on the blockchain using your wallet address. We recommend reviewing blockchain privacy practices before using crypto payment features.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of material changes via email or in-app notification. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
